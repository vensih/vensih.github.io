About the project

The idea was not originally mine. Fatjon Kuçi had already done something like this years before, quietly remapping a Monopoly set to Shkodra. When we played his version I was hooked and knew I wanted to take it further. I kept the concept, rewrote the locations, and built the design from the ground up with custom icons, a new board layout, and my own visual direction. For the icons I started with rough sketches, used AI to work out the shapes and proportions, then cleaned everything up in Illustrator. The Monopoly Figma Community Kit by Cammy Hogg gave me the board framework to build on, which kept things moving instead of rebuilding the structure from zero. Colors and composition were done by hand throughout in Illustrator and Photoshop.

Meet the Shkopoly Carp

KRAPI I SHKODRËS

Common Carp of Lake Shkodra

The mascot is not a cartoon fish, but the carp of Lake Shkodra, an iconic freshwater species often regarded locally as the king of the lake. Deeply tied to Shkodra's identity, the carp is more than part of the lake; it is part of the city's memory, cuisine, and family tradition. It is best known as the heart of Tavë Krapi, a traditional baked dish layered with onions, garlic, tomatoes, and dried plums. Rich, warm, and unmistakably Shkodran, it is the kind of meal served at a family table, shared slowly, and remembered long after it is finished.

Custom Icons

Shkodra instead of squares

Replaces Free Parking

Biçikleta

Shkodra is known as the City of Bicycles, with more bikes per capita than anywhere in Albania. The bike is not a transport choice here, it is the default.

Replaces In Jail

Pelikani Kaçurrel

The Dalmatian pelican breeds on Lake Shkodra, making it the city's unofficial emblem. The face says innocent, the bars say otherwise. Roll doubles to get out.

Replaces Go to Jail

Prokuroria

Shkodra's prosecution office takes the place of the classic board cop. Land here and someone is writing up the charges. If you end up in jail, you know who signed the papers.

Replaces Community Chest

Teatri Migjeni

Named after the poet Migjeni, born in Shkodra in 1911. The theatre has been the city's cultural anchor since 1949. Draw a card, hope the community is generous.

Replaces Train / Station

Urbani

April 2026 brought a full rehaul of Shkodra's urban bus network. New routes, new vehicles, redesigned for a modern city. The train station exists, but trains rarely stop.

Replaces Electric Company

OSHEE

Albania's electricity operator, running the grid across the country. Own this and opponents pay based on the dice. Just like real bills, you never know how high it goes.

Replaces Waterworks

TRUMA

Shkodra's groundwater is so abundant that many families skip the city line entirely, digging their own truma çezmë for fresh water. Own it and rents double anyway.

Replaces Luxury Tax

Napolon Ari

French 20-franc gold coins circulated as real currency in Shkodra for generations. Savings were kept in gold, not banks. Landing here costs you exactly that.
